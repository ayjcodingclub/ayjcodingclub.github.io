AYJ Coding Club Home

Coding club is a student-run club designed to get students involved in programming both as a career path and as a hobby. We encourage students to learn new languages, get involved, inspire others and have fun! Our goal is to create teams to join major hackathons, as well as host a few of our own to get comfortable. We're also a great space for coding questions and cs homework help!
